What is Manipal Facmash?

MIT Facemash is based on a website called facemash.com built by Mark Zuckerberg which compared two Harvard student pictures side-by-side and let visitors choose the hotter one. If you want to know more about Facemash, watch this amazing movie called The Social Network. Here is a small scene from the movie about Facemash.



How did you make it?

Very simple. Log in to your Websismit Portal. Right click on your display picture. Select open image in new tab. You will see something like this in your address bar.

Now modify the last few digits of your imgId and you will get a new picture. Works, right? Too easy. And if you run a simple loop, varying just the imgId values, you can get their entire picture database in your computer. For rating pictures, I used the Elo rating system, the same one used in the original Facemash.
